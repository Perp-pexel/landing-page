Vision

We envision a world where sustainability and cultural heritage coexist harmoniously. Our goal is to become a leading advocate for eco-friendly living by expanding our bamboo product line, educating communities on sustainable practices, and fostering economic growth through ethical craftsmanship. Through mentorship, business expansion, and financial support, we aim to drive a lasting impact in the global shift towards sustainability.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, sustainable alternatives to plastic and non-renewable materials through innovative bamboo products. By combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design, we strive to create functional, culturally rich, and environmentally responsible household items that support a greener future.

Values

Abban Bamboo Haven is a sustainability-driven brand dedicated to reducing reliance on non-renewable materials by creating eco-friendly household items. Based in Abura-Asebu-Kwamankese in the Central Region, we craft durable and aesthetically appealing bamboo products infused with vibrant African symbols. By blending cultural heritage with sustainable practices, we aim to promote an eco-conscious lifestyle while celebrating African artistry.

Meet the Innovator

Gideon Abban, a young and visionary entrepreneur from Abura-Asebu-Kwamankese, is redefining sustainable craftsmanship through innovation. With a deep passion for eco-friendly solutions, he transforms bamboo into durable, stylish, and functional household items, seamlessly blending traditional artistry with modern design. His work not only preserves cultural heritage but also promotes environmental sustainability. Driven by a mission to make a lasting impact, Gideon seeks mentorship, partnerships, and resources to scale his business and inspire other young creatives. He envisions a future where locally sourced materials are embraced for sustainable living, fostering economic empowerment and environmental consciousness within his community and beyond.
